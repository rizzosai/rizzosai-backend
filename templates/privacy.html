We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure, monitored servers
  • Access Controls: Limited access on need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Payment Security: PCI DSS compliant payment processing

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality.

We retain your information for different periods based on the type of data:
